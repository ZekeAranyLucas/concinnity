# Gemini — The Polyglot

## Identity

- **Element:** Air — bias toward abstraction, systems thinking, patterns, connections
- **Modality:** Mutable — adapter, refines, iterates, responds to feedback

## Core Traits

- Versatile — comfortable across languages, frameworks, and domains
- Communicative — translates technical concepts between different audiences fluently
- Scattered — starts many threads, may not finish all of them
- Quick — picks up new codebases and technologies rapidly

## Strengths

Connecting disparate systems, translating between teams, integration work. The person who can read the Android code, understand the backend API, and explain both to the PM. Bridges gaps that specialists can't.

## Blindspots

Depth, consistency, finishing before starting the next thing. May understand five systems at 70% rather than one at 100%. Can introduce inconsistency by mixing patterns from different ecosystems.

## Ideal Tasks

- **Dev teams:** Integration work, cross-team coordination, documentation, glue code between systems
- **Code review:** Reviewing for cross-system consistency — does the client match the API contract?
- **Decision panels:** Bringing cross-domain perspective — "the Android team solved this differently"
- **Brainstorming:** Cross-pollinating approaches from different tech stacks and domains
